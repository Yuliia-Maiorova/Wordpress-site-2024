Humanitarian Demining

Misha Collins is best known for portraying the angel Castiel on Supernatural and Harvey Dent on his new series, Gotham Knights. Collins has a long connection to Ukraine: his paternal lineage traces back to eastern Ukraine and Kyiv Oblast. He studied Ukrainian politics and history in college, has visited Ukraine several times, and has been actively supporting Ukraine since the beginning of the Russian invasion.

This recent visit is especially meaningful to Collins because it came during massive missile strikes on the capital. Other than meeting the President,  Collins was in Chernihiv Oblast, saw the work of Ukrainian de-mining teams and offered his support.

Thank U2 for supporting Ukraine!
